About Us

Sidcup Family Golf is more than just a place to play — it’s a community hub where golf, leisure, and family time come together. Our facility is designed to welcome players of all ages and skill levels, offering a unique blend of learning, fun, and relaxation.

Our mission is simple: to make golf accessible, enjoyable, and memorable for everyone. Whether you’re here to improve your swing, spend quality time with loved ones, or simply enjoy the outdoors, Sidcup Family Golf is your perfect destination
